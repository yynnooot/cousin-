About Tae

After majoring in Writing at Eugene Lang College at The New School, Tae began working at Food & Wine magazine, which kicked off his illustrious career in the food and restaurant industry. Since then, he's worked at renowned places such as Momofuku Ssam Bar, P.D.T., Sunny Spot, Khe-Yo, Suzume, Sempio, and Cocktail Kingdom. From being a front-of-house staff captain or restaurant office manager, to the first male door person of one of the world's most famous speakeasies or handling all marketing and communications for the the industry's #1 barware company, Tae's extensive professional experience within the food and restaurant industry has helped garner a true understanding of the business.

Tae's industry expertise and dedication to writing has been chronicled in his personal blog since 2006. He's also been a food, alcohol, and restaurant contributor to VICE since 2013, in addition to contributing to their food channel MUNCHIES since launching in 2014. His writing has also appeared in Gothamist.com and Edible Queens.

In the summer of 2017, Tae will be heading to Seoul in order to work on a book about the food and restaurant scene of South Korea's vibrant capital city.

He is currently in search of literary representation, and has a book proposal and samples of fiction upon request.
